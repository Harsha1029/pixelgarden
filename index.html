FREQUENTLY ASKED

HOW DO I CONNECT TO A FRIEND?

Share your unique Master ID from the Chat Screen. Once your friend pastes it and clicks connect, you'll be synced instantly for chatting, calling, and drawing.

IS THE CALLING FREE?

Yes! All calls are completely free. They happen directly between your browser and your friend's without any extra fees.

HOW DO I DRAW TOGETHER?

Once you are "Online" with a friend, click the Blue Pencil button in the sidebar. This will instantly take both of you to the Collaborative Board.

CAN I SEND IMAGES AND STICKERS?

Absolutely! Use the paperclip icon to attach images or videos, and the smiley face icon to choose from a variety of garden-themed stickers.

WHAT ARE GARDEN MASTERS?

Garden Masters are the elite protectors of the pixel garden. You can choose from 10 unique masters, each with their own legendary style.

IS IT MOBILE FRIENDLY?

Yes, the Pixel Garden is designed to work smoothly on both desktop and mobile browsers, so you can chat and draw on the go!

HOW IS MY CHAT DATA HANDLED?

Your chat data is completely private. Since we use a direct peer-to-peer connection, your messages go directly to your friend and are never stored on any central server.
